About Me

My name is Gopal Kundu and I am a senior mechanical design engineer with over 10 years of experience in the field. I specialize in the design and development of mechanical systems and components, utilizing advanced CAD software and Finite Element Analysis techniques. I am also proficient in 3D printing and rapid prototyping. I have a proven track record of delivering high-quality designs on schedule and within budget.

My experience includes working on various projects such as aerospace, automotive, medical devices and consumer products. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to improve my skills and contribute to the success of the projects and the company.
